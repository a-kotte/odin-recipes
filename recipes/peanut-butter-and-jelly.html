PB&J

Description

PB&J (Peanut butte and jelly) is a classic comfort sandwhich that can be made in less than 5 minutes and fill you with joy!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons jam or jelly of choice

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly or jam on the other slice of bread.
  3. Combine slices to make a sandwich and enjoy!
