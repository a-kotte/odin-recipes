Raita

Description

Raita is an Indian yogurt dish that often accompanies other dishes (like Biryani or Aloo Paratha). It is a healthy and cooling side dish that will surely enhance your meal!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Chopped Cucumber
  • 1/2 Chopped Tomato
  • 1 Chopped Green Chili
  • 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt)
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera)
  • 1/2 tablespoon Red Chili Powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped Cilanto (Coriander / Dhania) Leaves

Steps

  1. First, roast the cumin seeds until fragrant and browned. Once roasted, remove intoa bowl to cool.
  2. Then grind the roasted cumin seeds in a mortar with a pestle.
  3. Now whisk the yogurt in a bowl and add the chopped cucumbers, tomatoes, chili, roasted cumin powder, chili pwder, salt, and cilantro leaves. Stir well to combine and serve!
