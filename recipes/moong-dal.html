Moong Dal

Description

Moong Dal is a yellow lentil curry that can be eaten with rice or bread. It is protein packed, comforting, easy to make, and healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1/4 (1 small) cup onions
  • 1/2 cup (1 large) tomato
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ginger peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 green chili
  • 4-5 curry leaves
  • 1/8 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 3/4-2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

Steps

  1. Pour oil into pressure cooker. When oil is hot, add cumin seeds. Once cumin seeds begin to splutter, add ginger, green chilis, and curry leaves.
  2. Fry until aromatic (about a minute) then add onions. Saute until transparent or pink, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and salt. Saute for 1-2 minutes. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Saute for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Drain the water from the moong dal and add them. Pour water and mix well to deglaze bottom of the cooker.
  5. Cover and pressure cook for 4-5 whistles on a medium flame.
  6. When pressure drops, open lid and add kasuri methi. If preferred, dal can be mashed. Salt to taste, and serve warm!
